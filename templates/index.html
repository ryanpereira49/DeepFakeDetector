What are DeepFakes?

Deepfakes are images or videos that are synthetically manipulated or generated using deep learning. This technology can be used to create large amounts of misinformation, damage reputations, and harm individuals.

How are DeepFakes detected?

There are various detection techniques avaliable such as looking for artifacts on the face, tracking eye movement, using deep learning to train machines to detect DeepFakes.
